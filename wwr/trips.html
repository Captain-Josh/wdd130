Scenic Family Float (Class I-II)

This is a calm and relaxing rafting experience perfect for families, beginners, and guests who want to enjoy beautiful river scenery without fast rapids. It's a great way to introduce children or first-time rafters to the water while still having fun and learning basic paddling skills.

  • Gentle currents ideal for all ages
  • Professional guide steering the raft
  • Great opportunities for photos and sightseeing
  • Includes paddle, life jacket, and optional splash jacket

Adventure Canyon Run (Class II-III)

This trip offers a perfect blend of scenic beauty and splashy excitement. With moderate rapids and wave trains, it's designed for guests who want more action while still keeping things accessible and fun for most skill levels.

  • Moderate rapids with lively waves
  • Safety briefing and instruction before launch
  • Ideal for adventurous beginners
  • Includes helmet, paddle, and life jacket

Whitewater Challenge (Class III-IV)

This experience delivers a thrilling whitewater adventure with stronger rapids, faster currents, and more technical paddling. Perfect for guests seeking adrenaline and a physically engaging challenge on the river.

  • Exciting drops and maneuvering sections
  • Led by experienced, certified guides
  • Suitable for fit and active participants
  • Includes helmet, paddle, wetsuit (cold days), and life jacket

Full Day Rapid Expedition (Mixed Class I-IV)

A full day trip designed for adventurers who want to explore multiple sections of the river, combining calm stretches with energetic rapids. This experience includes breaks, meals, and a chance to see stunning natural landscapes throughout the day.

  • Mix of calm floats and exciting rapid sections
  • Lunch and rest breaks included
  • Great for groups and thrill seekers
  • Includes paddle, helmet, life jacket, and dry bag
